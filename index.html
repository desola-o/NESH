Get in touch

What areas in Nigeria do you serve?

We are headquartered in Lekki, Lagos and primarily serve clients across Lagos State and surrounding areas. Depending on the scope of the project, we can also travel to other states. Contact us to discuss your location and specific needs.

Do you offer one-off services or ongoing contracts?

We offer both. Whether you need a one-time deep clean, a single interior design project, or a long-term facility management contract, we tailor our services to match your exact requirements and budget.

How quickly can you mobilise a team?

We pride ourselves on rapid response. For urgent requests — such as emergency cleaning or short-notice project support — we can typically mobilise a team within 24–48 hours depending on location and scope.

Do you work with contractors and developers?

Absolutely. Project support for contractors is one of our core service lines. We help you add the final touch of excellence to your projects — from interior finishing and cleaning to procurement — so you hand over with confidence.

Can NESH handle multiple buildings or locations?

Yes. We are set up to manage multiple properties simultaneously, delivering the same consistent standard of service whether you have one building or an entire portfolio. We scale with your business.

How do I request a consultation or quote?

Simply fill in the contact form below, send us a WhatsApp message, or give us a call. We'll schedule a consultation to understand your needs and provide a detailed, obligation-free quote.
